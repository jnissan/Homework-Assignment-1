Characters

Toby

Toby is a charismatic 17 year old, living in a well-off Confine in the Northeastern sector of North America. He is actively questioning the relationship of his Confine with the people he knows are living outside the walls. What he wants most is to see the world beyond the walls and to learn more about what caused everything to change.

Kellan

Kellan is Toby's best friend. He is as curious as they come and always up for an adventure. He is a notrious trouble maker within the Confine and has a very hard time staying out of trouble, much to his single mother's dismay.

Heather

Heather is Toby and Kellan's classmate and a popular girl inside the Confine. She hasn't had to ask for much and her father will stop at nothing to make sure she's safe.
